Vehicle Contracts

Vehicle smart contracts are blockchain-based contracts that automate and secure various aspects of vehicle-related transactions and

processes. When a new vehicle is added to the system, a smart contract can record the vehicle's details, including its VIN (Vehicle Identification Number), make, model, and owner's information on the blockchain.

User Contracts

Client smart contracts in a vehicle management system refer to blockchain-based contracts designed to facilitate and automate various interactions and

transactions between vehicle owners or users (the clients) and the vehicle management system.

Insurance Verifications

An insurance verification smart contract in a vehicle management system is a blockchain-based contract designed to automate the process of verifying insurance

coverage for vehicles within the system. This smart contract plays a crucial role in ensuring that vehicles are adequately insured before they are driven or rented, enhancing safety and compliance.

Maintenance Records

Maintenance records in a smart contract can be implemented to keep a transparent and tamper-proof record of maintenance activities for vehicles. These records can be beneficial for fleet management,

vehicle ownership, and various other automotive applications. The smart contract can store vehicle information, including the Vehicle Identification Number (VIN), make, model, owner's information, and other relevant details.

Accident Records

Accident record smart contracts in a vehicle management system can help maintain transparent and tamper-proof records of accidents involving vehicles. These records are valuable for insurance claims,

legal purposes, and overall safety management. The smart contract stores vehicle information, including the Vehicle Identification Number (VIN), make, model, owner's information, and other relevant details.

Theft Records

Theft record smart contracts in a vehicle management system can help maintain secure and tamper-proof records of stolen or recovered vehicles. These smart contracts are valuable tools for law enforcement agencies,

insurance companies, and vehicle owners to track and report theft incidents.These contracts can also store the theft status of a vehicle, indicating whether it has been recovered or is still missing. Authorized parties can update this status when new information becomes available.

ownership transfer

An ownership transfer smart contract in a vehicle management system is a blockchain-based contract designed to automate and secure the process of transferring ownership of vehicles from one party to another.

This type of smart contract can be particularly valuable in scenarios such as buying or selling vehicles, leasing, or transferring ownership within a fleet management system.
